Jem Stevens

Course Manager - Plumbing

Jem has travelled the world as an airframes aircraft engineer in the Royal Air Force, he then retrained as a plumbing and gas heating engineer and worked in the construction industry for over a decade before joining Cornwall College. Jem is also a qualified PASMA scaffold instructor and delivers scaffold training to our students enabling them to gain a nationally recognised PASMA accreditation.
