Childcare

If you have young children we can provide you with support so that you can achieve your qualifications and career goals.

You will be eligible for financial support towards your childcare costs if you have a household income of less than £21,000 (£25,000 for students in reciept of an Advanced Learning Loan).

To apply, please download and complete the two forms at the bottom of this page.

Under 20?
If you are under 20 at the start of your course, you can apply for funding to help your childcare costs.
Care to Learn has been set up by the government to help pay for your childcare and related travel costs while you are learning.

Letting someone else look after you child can be a big step!
Care to Learn helps by letting you choose the type of childcare that suits you and your child. You can, for example, use it to pay for childcare at any of the following places

  • Childminder
  • Pre‐school playgroup
  • Day nursery
  • Out of school club
  • Other registered childcare

Childcare providers must be registered with Ofsted, meet national standards and may be inspected to make sure they are providing good quality care.
Find more information visit gov.uk/care-to-learn
Or call Directgov’s Learner Support helpline on 0800 121 8989.

Equine

The Equestrian Centre provides excellent teaching and learning facilities for full and part‐time students and external customers. See the section on Equine courses for more information.

The facilities include an extended 20m x 61m indoor arena with a Wax Pro surface with mirrors, gallery seating for 148 people, two state of the art teaching rooms, judging areas and staff offices/reception area. In addition there is a 30 x 60 Outdoor arena, 2 full sets of show jumps, Music and PA system, XC‐Schooling field, Walker, Weighbridge and Solarium. There are 2 separate yards consisting of 14 traditional stables adjacent to the Indoor School and 24 internal loose boxes in an American Style Barn.

We offer a range of Livery options for Students, from Working Livery to Full Livery. Our team of highly qualified experienced staff are on hand to offer their expertise. If you are interested in booking the facilities or livery options, please contact using the information below.

Our Stoke Climsland campus is home to an industry approved Equestrian Centre with extensive stabling and is equipped with two indoor arenas and large outdoor arena, a weighbridge, horse walker, wash down and solarium facilities as well as state‐of‐the‐art equipment and technology, all of which are used extensively by students. With excellent links to employers, practitioners and researchers, nationally and internationally, our students have the opportunity to train and develop their skills for employment in all aspects of the equestrian industry.

Duchy College is also home to the Equitation Science Research and Training Centre which works closely with the International Society for Equitation Science. Our students also benefit from comprehensive links with leading equine practitioners such as equine nutritionists, organisations, charities (such as The Horse Trust), veterinary professionals, competitors and equitation science practitioners.

Rosewarne Nursery

/rosewarnenurseries
e-mail us
07748920489

Rosewarne Nursery began in the early 1990s as an educational facility and quickly grew into a commercial enterprise supplying the nursery trade throughout Cornwall and Devon, which it is still doing to this day.

We are now supplying the majority of the garden centres across Cornwall including Carnon Downs, Chacewater and Trevena Cross, as well as having prestigious clients such as the Eden Project, Lost Gardens of Heligan and the National Trust.

Sustainability and environmental issues are always at the forefront of our work here, for example, using peat-free compost and relying on borehole water to supply the nursery. It is this ethos that has encouraged the likes of Eden and the National Trust to work closely with us.

We supply a broad variety of plants including Southern Hemisphere plants, coastal plants and a good range of hardy shrubs, grasses and herbaceous varieties focussing on the landscape trade. We also grow and supply a huge number of Agapanthus Rosewarne, which was bred in the early 1990s and still proves to be a really good all round and hardy agapanthus and one of our best-sellers.

The nursery is still a valuable educational resource and we endeavour to keep the nursery to a high standard of quality and cleanliness to show how a wholesale nursery should be run, encouraging best practice in our students. It is run by a small, dedicated and knowledgeable team, led by Nursery Manager John Ashton.

If you want to visit the nursery, please contact us in advance.

Dog Grooming

12:00 - 13:00
e-mail us
01209 611659

Duchy College, Rosewarne’s Dog Grooming Salon boasts a range of high quality, state of the art equipment for the students to use whilst training.

The salon has two stainless steel baths, one “easy-step” manual bath and the other a remote controlled, electrically adjusted bath. There are six electric tables to suit a range of dogs from Yorkshire Terriers to Giant Schnauzers plus a wide variety of restraint equipment and crates to help the groomer and keep the dogs safe and secure whilst in the team’s care. Blasters, stand and tornado dryers are available, which are both mobile and directional for ease of coat drying after bathing. The salon team use a wide range of professional shampoos and conditioners for each, unique coat type and colour. Each student has access to brushing, scissoring, clipping and mat removal equipment or staff can advise where to purchase a student pack of equipment should they prefer to use their own.

The salon is bright, roomy and has high level air conditioning, sealed, easy to clean flooring and walls and a viewing window for others to observe the busy lessons. The lecturers and trainers are industry experts and qualified in both teaching and dog grooming and the Course Manager is a registered Dog Behaviour Trainer as well as a dog groomer. The welfare and safety of the dogs are taken into account at all times thus creating a relaxed environment so both dogs and students feel at ease throughout the process.

The award winning Trevenson Restaurant is located at Cornwall College’s Camborne campus and is one of two fine dining commercial restaurants within the Cornwall College Group.

Open Monday to Friday for lunch and some Wednesday evenings (term time), the award winning Trevenson Restaurant is one of the two fine dining commercial restaurants within the Cornwall College Group. Opened by Michelin star chefs Nathan Outlaw and Pierre Koffmann, Trevenson offers a very special dining experience, offering both creative and mouth‐watering menus every day.

Recent winners of a prestigious Highly Commended AA College Rosette, the ethos to Trevenson is simple; continually driven by sustainable seasonality, using locally sourced ingredients, we ensure ever changing gastronomic dishes, freshly prepared in our state‐of‐the‐art kitchens & delivered by an attentive and enthusiastic team. The Hospitality & Catering Department also holds 3 Centre’s of Excellence including Fish & Shellfish, Food & Beverage Service and Patisserie & Confectionary as well as the coveted title by People 1st of Gold Accreditation, this makes us one of the best catering college in the UK, evident by the standard of food on our menus and the attention to detail in our service.

Trevenson Restaurant not only serve traditional classic dishes, they also serve new and exciting dishes to cater for all tastes which are devised by our professional staff and experienced students.

The stylish interior is akin to any fine dining establishment, offering a relaxing atmosphere for informal lunch, as well as a contemporary ambience for evening fine diners.

Trevenson has also been awarded the Gold Cornwall Healthier Eating and Food Safety (CHEFS) Award two years running, making it only one of five restaurants in the county to receive the accolade, something we are all truly proud of.

To dine at Trevenson is a wonderful experience and as well as our a la carte menu, we also offer tasting menus and special themed evenings, where we invite local established chefs to devise, prepare and host selected events, all supported by our staff and students. To find out about our next themed evening, simply contact the reservations number below.

Trevenson is fully licensed and offers a comprehensive selection of wines and beers, carefully chosen to accompany each dish on the menu.
